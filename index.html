1. Introduction

This blog is a collection of pictures I took during my exchange semester in Morocco in order to show the authentic side of this country. Through this project, I want to show both sides of Morocco that the media doesn't always reveal. Also, I want to help students that are new to Morrocco to discover the best places.

2. Me

I am a 3rd year BBA Student at ESSEC Cergy and went on an exchange semester in ESSEC Africa. At first, I was feeling lost in this new country so I did this website to help new students there get familiar with the campus, houses, and places to visit
